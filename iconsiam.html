ICON SIAM

Highlight: ICONSIAM is one of Bangkok’s most iconic and luxurious riverside shopping destinations, located along the Chao Phraya River. Known as a “mega lifestyle complex,” ICONSIAM combines world-class shopping, dining, culture, and entertainment into one spectacular destination. The mall features an extensive collection of global luxury brands such as Hermès, Louis Vuitton, Gucci, Chanel, and Cartier, attracting high-end shoppers from around the world. Beyond luxury retail, ICONSIAM offers a unique cultural experience through its famous indoor floating market, where visitors can explore authentic Thai street food and traditional products in a beautifully designed riverside setting. The complex also includes fine dining restaurants, international cuisine, exhibition spaces, and entertainment venues. One of its highlights is the stunning riverside promenade, which provides breathtaking views of the Chao Phraya River, especially in the evening. With its grand architecture, innovative concept, and seamless blend of modern luxury and Thai cultural heritage, ICONSIAM is more than just a shopping mall—it is a global landmark and a must-visit destination that represents the new face of Bangkok.

Time: 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM

Transport: BTS Charoen Nakhon / Shuttle boat
