JODD FAIR RATCHADA

Highlight: JODD FAIRS is one of Bangkok’s most popular modern night markets, known for its trendy atmosphere and vibrant street food scene. It offers a wide variety of local Thai dishes, international street food, and unique snacks that attract both locals and tourists. The market is also famous for its Instagram-worthy stalls, colorful lighting, and creative food presentations, such as giant ribs, seafood buckets, and dessert cafes. Beyond food, JODD FAIRS features fashion stalls, handmade products, and lifestyle items at affordable prices. Its lively environment, live music, and youthful crowd make it a perfect place to experience Bangkok’s nightlife in a casual and fun way.

Time: 05:00 PM – 12:00 AM

Transport: MRT Thailand Cultural Centre Station Exit 4
