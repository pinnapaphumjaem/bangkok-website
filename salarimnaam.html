SALA RIM NAAM RESTAURANT (THAI)

Highlight: Sala Rim Naam is one of Bangkok’s most prestigious Thai restaurants, offering a luxurious riverside dining experience along the Chao Phraya River. Located opposite the renowned Mandarin Oriental Bangkok, the restaurant is set in a beautifully designed traditional Thai pavilion, surrounded by lush gardens and serene waterfront views. What makes Sala Rim Naam truly special is not only its authentic Thai cuisine but also its immersive cultural experience. Guests can enjoy classical Thai dance performances accompanied by live traditional music, creating a refined and memorable atmosphere. The menu features a wide selection of classic Thai dishes, including rich curries, spicy salads, stir-fried specialties, and exquisite desserts, all prepared with premium ingredients and elegant presentation.

Time: 06:00 PM – 10:30 PM

Price: 1,000–3,000+ THB per person

Direction:

BTS Saphan Taksin (Exit 2), take the hotel’s complimentary shuttle boat across the Chao Phraya River.
