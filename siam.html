SIAM PARAGON

Highlight: Siam Paragon is one of the most luxurious and prestigious shopping malls in Thailand, located in the heart of Bangkok’s prime shopping district. It is widely recognized for its exceptional retail experience, featuring an impressive collection of global luxury brands such as Louis Vuitton, Gucci, Chanel, Prada, and Dior, making it a premier destination for high-end shopping enthusiasts. Beyond its luxury retail offerings, Siam Paragon provides a complete lifestyle experience. The mall features a world-class gourmet food hall, fine dining restaurants, and a wide range of international cuisines. It is also home to SEA LIFE Bangkok Ocean World, one of the largest aquariums in Southeast Asia, offering an immersive underwater experience. In addition, visitors can enjoy a state-of-the-art cinema, exhibition spaces, and entertainment zones that regularly host international events. With its elegant architecture, premium atmosphere, and diverse attractions, Siam Paragon is more than just a shopping mall—it represents Bangkok’s modern luxury lifestyle and stands as a must-visit destination for shopping, dining, and entertainment.

Time: 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM

Transport: BTS Siam (Exit 5)
