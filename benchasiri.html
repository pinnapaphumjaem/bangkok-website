BENCHASIRI PARK

Highlight: Benchasiri Park is a compact yet lively park located in the Sukhumvit area, right next to Emporium shopping mall. Despite its smaller size, the park is beautifully designed with modern sculptures, a central pond, and well-maintained jogging paths. It is particularly popular among locals for exercise, especially in the evening when the park becomes vibrant with runners, families, and people engaging in outdoor fitness activities. Its central location makes it a convenient green space for relaxation in one of Bangkok’s busiest districts.

Time: 05:00 AM – 09:00 PM

Transport: BTS Phrom Phong
