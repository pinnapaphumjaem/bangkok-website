TRAIN NIGHT MARKET (SRINAKARIN)

Highlight: Train Night Market (Ratchada) is known for its colorful tent layout and lively night market vibe. It offers a mix of street food, vintage items, bars, and fashion stalls, making it popular among young people and tourists. The market is especially famous for its rooftop views, where visitors can see rows of colorful tents glowing at night—one of Bangkok’s most iconic night market scenes.

Time: 05:00 PM – 01:00 AM

Transport: MRT Si Nut
