ASIATIQUE THE RIVER FRONT

Highlight: Asiatique The Riverfront is a unique open-air night market and lifestyle destination located along the Chao Phraya River. Combining shopping, dining, and entertainment, it offers a more organized and scenic market experience compared to traditional street markets. Visitors can explore boutique shops, souvenir stores, and enjoy riverside dining with beautiful views. One of its main attractions is the large Ferris wheel, which provides panoramic views of the river and city skyline, especially at night.

Time: 05:00 PM – 12:00 AM

Transport: BTS Saphan Taksin
