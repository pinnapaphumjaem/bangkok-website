KHAO SAN ROAD

Highlight: Khao San Road is a world-famous backpacker street known for its energetic nightlife, street food, bars, and budget shopping. It is a hub for international travelers, offering a mix of cultures, music, and entertainment. Visitors can enjoy street performances, try exotic foods, shop for souvenirs, and experience Bangkok’s party atmosphere all in one place.

Time: 06:00 PM – 02:00 AM

Direction:

MRT Sam Yot (Exit 1)
