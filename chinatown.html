CHINATOWN

Highlight: Chinatown (Yaowarat) is one of the most vibrant and historic districts in Bangkok, famous for its bustling street food scene and rich Chinese heritage. At night, the streets come alive with neon lights, food stalls, and endless culinary options ranging from seafood and dim sum to desserts and traditional Chinese dishes. It is also a cultural hub where visitors can explore temples, gold shops, and traditional markets. The energetic atmosphere, combined with authentic flavors and cultural richness, makes Chinatown a must-visit destination for food lovers.

Time: 04:00 PM – 02:00 AM

Transport: MRT Wat Mangkon
