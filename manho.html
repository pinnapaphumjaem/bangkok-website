MAN HO CHAINESE RESTAURANT

Highlight: Man Ho is a premium Chinese restaurant specializing in Cantonese cuisine, offering an elegant dining experience with high-quality ingredients and refined flavors. Located in a luxury hotel setting, the restaurant is known for its sophisticated ambiance and attentive service. Signature dishes include dim sum, roasted duck, seafood, and traditional Cantonese delicacies prepared by experienced chefs. The restaurant emphasizes freshness and presentation, making it ideal for both casual dining and special occasions. With its combination of luxury, authentic flavors, and excellent service, Man Ho is a top choice for Chinese cuisine in Bangkok.

Time: 11:30 AM – 10:00 PM

Price: 800–2,000 THB/person

Transport: BTS Asok
