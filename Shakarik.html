SHAKARIKI 432 ASOKE

Highlight: Shakariki 432 Asoke is a popular Japanese izakaya-style restaurant known for its lively atmosphere and authentic Osaka-style dishes. Open late, it offers a wide variety of Japanese comfort food such as grilled skewers, ramen, sushi, and beer, making it a favorite spot for nightlife dining.

Time: 05:00 PM – 11:00 PM

Price: 200–600 THB/person

Transport: • BTS Asok – 3–5 minute walk

• MRT Sukhumvit – 3–5 minute walk
