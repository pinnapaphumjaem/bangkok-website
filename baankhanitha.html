BAAN KHANITHA

Highlight: Baan Khanitha is a well-known Thai restaurant that offers an authentic and refined dining experience in a traditional Thai house setting. The restaurant is famous for its elegant atmosphere, combining classic Thai architecture with warm hospitality, making it popular among both locals and international visitors. The menu features a wide selection of traditional Thai dishes prepared with high-quality ingredients and beautifully presented. Signature dishes include spicy soups, curries, and stir-fried specialties that highlight the rich flavors of Thai cuisine. Baan Khanitha is especially known for maintaining authentic recipes while elevating them to a fine-dining level. With its peaceful ambiance, excellent service, and authentic taste, Baan Khanitha is a perfect place to experience traditional Thai cuisine in a sophisticated environment.

Time: 11:00 AM – 11:00 PM

Price: ~300–800 THB/person

Transport: BTS Asok
