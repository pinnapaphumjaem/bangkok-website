THE GOVERNOR (HITON BANGKOK GRANDE ASOKE)

Highlight: Located on Level 2 of Hilton Bangkok Grande Asoke, The Governor is where North American diner nostalgia meets the vibrant energy of a downtown sports bar. Serving up bold comfort food and chill vibes, this lively hangout is the go-to spot for juicy gourmet burgers, BBQ pork ribs, Sriracha-braised short ribs, crispy fries, and classic root beer floats elevated with premium ingredients and punchy flavors. Whether you're dropping by for a quick lunch, winding down after work, or enjoying a casual family dinner, The Governor dishes out fun and flavor in every bite. The beverage menu is just as exciting, offering everything from nostalgic and kid-friendly floats to boozy versions for grown-ups, plus a hand-selected range of Thai craft beers.

12.00 PM - 02.00 AM

Price: 400–900 THB/person

Direction:

BTS Asok (Exit 3)/ MRT Sukhumvit (Exit 2)(walk 5–10 miniutes)
