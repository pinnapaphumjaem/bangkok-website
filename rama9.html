CENTRAL RAMA 9

Highlight: Central Rama 9 is a modern lifestyle shopping mall located in Bangkok’s rapidly growing business district of Rama 9, often considered part of the city’s “New CBD.” The mall is popular among office workers, students, and local residents, offering a convenient and well-rounded shopping experience with a mix of fashion, technology, dining, and entertainment. Central Rama 9 features a wide range of international and high-street brands such as Uniqlo, H&M, Zara, Nike, and Adidas, along with lifestyle and tech stores that cater to everyday urban needs. The mall also includes supermarkets and convenience-focused outlets, making it a practical destination for daily shopping. In addition to retail, the mall offers a variety of dining options, from casual eateries to popular restaurant chains and international cuisine. It also features a cinema complex and entertainment areas, providing a space for relaxation after work or study. Its direct connection to MRT Phra Ram 9 Station makes it highly accessible and convenient for commuters. With its strategic location, diverse retail mix, and everyday convenience, Central Rama 9 represents the modern urban lifestyle of Bangkok and serves as a key shopping and social hub in the city’s new business district.

Time: 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM

Transport: MRT Phra Ram 9 (Exit 1)
