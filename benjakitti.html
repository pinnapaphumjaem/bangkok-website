BENJAKITTI PARK

Highlight: Benjakitti Park is a modern urban park that stands out for its expansive lake and contemporary landscape design. Originally developed on the site of a former tobacco factory, the park has been transformed into a large green space that emphasizes sustainability and ecological balance. One of its most distinctive features is the elevated skywalk, which allows visitors to walk through lush wetlands and observe native plants and wildlife from above. The park also includes dedicated cycling tracks, jogging paths, and open areas for relaxation. Its location near Bangkok’s central business district makes it a popular destination for office workers and city dwellers seeking a break from their daily routine. The park is particularly stunning during sunset, when the skyline reflects over the water, creating a picturesque urban landscape that blends nature with modern architecture.

Time: 05:00 AM – 09:00 PM

Transport: MRT Queen Sirikit NCC
