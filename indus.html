INDUS RESTAURANT (INDIA)

Highlight: Indus is a renowned Indian fine-dining restaurant set in a beautiful colonial-style house surrounded by lush greenery. It offers a luxurious and tranquil dining atmosphere that stands out from the busy city environment. The menu features authentic North Indian cuisine, including rich curries, tandoori dishes, and freshly baked naan bread. The use of aromatic spices and traditional cooking techniques creates deep and flavorful dishes that are both authentic and refined. Indus is especially popular for romantic dinners and special occasions, offering a premium dining experience with elegant presentation and excellent service.

Time: 05:00 PM – 11:00 PM

Price: 800–2,000 THB/person

Transport: BTS Phrom Phong
