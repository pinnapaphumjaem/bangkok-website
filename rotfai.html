WATCHITABENCHATHAT PARK (SUAN ROT FAI)

Highlight: Wachirabenchathat Park, commonly known as Suan Rot Fai (Railway Park), is one of the largest and most peaceful parks in Bangkok. It is especially known for its well-maintained botanical gardens, wide cycling paths, and open green spaces that are perfect for outdoor recreation. The park offers a quieter and more natural atmosphere compared to other city parks, making it ideal for families, cyclists, and nature enthusiasts. Visitors can rent bicycles, explore the gardens, or simply relax in the calm surroundings. Its spacious layout allows for a more private and less crowded experience.

Time: 05:00 AM – 09:00 PM

Transport: BTS Mo Chit
