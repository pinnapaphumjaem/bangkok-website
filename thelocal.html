THE LOCAL BY OAM THONG THAI CUISINE

Highlight: The Local is a Michelin-recommended Thai restaurant that focuses on preserving traditional Thai recipes from different regions of Thailand. Set in a charming vintage house, the restaurant offers a cozy and nostalgic atmosphere that reflects Thai heritage. The menu features rare and regional dishes that are not commonly found in typical restaurants, such as southern curries, northern-style dishes, and unique appetizers. Each dish is carefully prepared using traditional cooking methods and authentic ingredients, providing a truly local culinary experience. With its unique concept, cultural authenticity, and high-quality food, The Local is a must-visit for those who want to explore deeper into Thai cuisine.

Time: 11:30 AM – 10:30 PM

Price: 400–1,000 THB/person

Transport: BTS Asok
