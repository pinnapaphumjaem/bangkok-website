CHATUCHAK MARKET

Highlight: Chatuchak Weekend Market is one of the largest markets in the world, featuring over 15,000 stalls selling everything from clothing and accessories to antiques, home décor, and street food. It is a paradise for shoppers looking for variety and unique items at affordable prices. The market is divided into sections, making it easier to explore different categories. It is especially busy on weekends, attracting both locals and tourists.

Time: • Fri: 06:00 PM – 12:00 AM • Weekend: 09:00 AM – 06:00 PM

Transport: BTS Mo Chit / MRT Kamphaeng Phet
